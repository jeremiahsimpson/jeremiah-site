Meet Hickory Mertsching

Hickory Mertsching is a brilliant local painter that just dropped off two new pieces at the shop. His work will be a fixture in our space because his sensibility is in total alignment with the Beam & Anchor aesthetic. His still lifes include birds, skulls, tools, plants and industrial detritus. All of these elements are in part inspired by the landscape in Wisconsin where he grew up and the Northwestern landscape that he now calls home.
